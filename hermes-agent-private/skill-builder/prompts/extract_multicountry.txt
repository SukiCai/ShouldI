## CRITICAL: Country Scope Tagging

Every insight you extract MUST include a `country_scope` field:
- `"universal"` — applies regardless of which country the student is in
- `"us"` — specific to the United States (OPT, H-1B, EB series, USCIS, etc.)
- `"canada"` — specific to Canada (PGWP, Express Entry, PNP, IRCC, etc.)
- `"australia"` — specific to Australia (if present in source)
- `"uk"` — specific to the UK (if present in source)

Rule of thumb: decision frameworks and mental models → `universal`. Visa names, quotas, processing times, specific rules → country code.
